### 2. Tagging Strategy

**Primary Tags:**
- `strategic-ad-intelligence-subscriber` (triggers sequence)
- `saas-500k-2m-arr` (target audience)
- `assessment-completed` (diagnostic tool completion)
- `consultation-interested` (soft conversion intent)
- `consultation-booked` (hard conversion)

**Lead Scoring Tags:**
- `high-engagement` (opens 5+ emails)
- `strategic-ready` (clicks assessment + consultation links)
- `enterprise-ready` (clicks case study + framework links)

### 3. Segmentation Rules

```javascript
// High-Intent Segment
Conditions: 
- Has tag "assessment-completed" 
- AND clicked "consultation" link
- AND revenue range $500K-$2M ARR

// Nurture Segment  
Conditions:
- Has tag "strategic-ad-intelligence-subscriber"
- AND NOT tagged "consultation-booked"
- AND engagement score > 50%

// Re-engagement Segment
Conditions:
- Has tag "strategic-ad-intelligence-subscriber" 
- AND last opened email > 7 days ago
- AND NOT tagged "consultation-booked"
```
