  • CAC increasing from $45 to $78

Their Previous Process: 2 weeks of brainstorming → 1 week of creative production → 1 week of testing → disappointing results → repeat.

Our 48-Hour Process:

Scraped 500+ competitor ads, identified 12 psychological patterns performing above baseline

Hour 9-24: Customer Language Research

Analyzed 2,000+ customer reviews, identified 3 emotional triggers with highest resonance

Hour 25-40: AI Creative Generation

Generated 50 hook variations, AI scored all concepts, selected top 5 performers

Hour 41-48: Performance Optimization

Refined winning concepts, created testing framework, delivered final creative brief

Winning Hook: "I used to avoid mirrors at work... now my coworkers ask what I changed"

AI Performance Score: 24/25 | Psychological Triggers: Social proof, transformation, specificity

The Secret Sauce: It wasn't just the winning hook. Our AI system identified the exact emotional pathway that resonated with their customers, then generated variations that all leveraged that same psychology.
